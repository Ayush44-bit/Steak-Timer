RARE STEAK

Cooking Instructions

Cook for 1.5–2 minutes per side on high heat. Outside seared, inside bright red and cool. Internal Temperature should be 120–125°F (49–52°C)

Resting Time

Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute.

Total Time

Approximately 9–11 minutes (including resting).

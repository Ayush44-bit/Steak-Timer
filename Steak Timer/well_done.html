WELL DONE STEAK

Cooking Instructions

Cook for 6–7 minutes per side on medium-low heat to avoid burning. No pink inside. The internal temperature should reach 160–170°F (71–77°C).

Resting Time

Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute.

Total Time

Approximately 17–19 minutes (including resting).

MEDIUM RARE STEAK

Cooking Instructions

Cook for 3–4 minutes per side on medium-high heat. Center will be warm and red. The internal temperature should reach 130–135°F (54–57°C).

Resting Time

Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute.

Total Time

Approximately 11-13 minutes (including resting time).

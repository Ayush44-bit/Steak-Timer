MEDIUM WELL STEAK

Cooking Instructions

Cook the steak for 5-6 minutes on each side at a medium temperature. Slight pink only in the center. The internal temperature should reach 150–155°F (66–68°C)

Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before serving to allow the juices to redistribute.

Total Time

Approximately 15-17 minutes (including resting time).
